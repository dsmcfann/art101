About Me

Ah, so we meet at last... the stars above foretold your arrival many years ago, of course, but to see you here, now...

But of course, where are my manners? My name is Donovan Sayegh-McFann. Before you came here, I was an Anthropology and Education, Democracy, and Justice double major at the University of California, Santa Cruz. Though I pursued my hobbies in music, puppetry, I had a secret craving that I longed to fulfill:

A craving for the arcane arts of HTML coding and web design.

Despite the judgement of my fellows, and the pleading of my loved ones, I delved deep into the murky darkness of HTML, CSS and JS. I learned how to apply text to a webpage, use my powers to make it appear BIG or small, just from the brush of my fingers. I felt the coding power surging through my finger tips. There was nothing I could not do: I was unstoppable.

But yet, all good things must come to an end. In a moment of hubris, while attempting to use the most powerful of HTML spells, I inadvertently captured m y s e l f onto the webpage. Despite my attempts to escape, I had already pushed myself from my local repository to the Net. I was trapped. This is all that remains of me...

But wait... perhaps...

you could help me?

Before I uploaded myself, I had backed up all of my work exploring these arcane arts to this webpage in the forms of "labs". If you were to explore them, to click on each of the links on the left, perhaps you could find a way to help me? Or, at least, you could learn from my folly on your own journey through the arcane.

The choice is yours. In the meantime, I think I will continue to rest here on this homepage. Take care of yourself, my friend. And good luck.
